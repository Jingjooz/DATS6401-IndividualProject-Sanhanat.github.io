G20 COUNTRIES

HEALTHCARE, MILITARY, EDUCATION

Welcome to the official G20 Government Expeditures Data website. This website will provide you with information about education, healthcare and military spending by data visualization. To create data visualization, we used the G20 spending data, collected yearly from 2012-2016, from World Bank Researchers, data analysts, economists and students can use the information for the better understanding of the G20 spending data.

To explore the data, click on the "OVERVIEW", "COMPARISON" or "TREND"
